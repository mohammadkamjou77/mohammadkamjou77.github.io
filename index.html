Profile

Mohammadreza Kamjou is an electrical engineer specializing in RF and analog integrated circuit design. He received his B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from CATC (2016–2020) and is currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Integrated Circuit Design at Sharif University of Technology (2023–2025). His technical expertise includes IC and RFIC design, analog circuit design, and EDA tools such as Cadence Virtuoso and ADS. His primary research focus is the design of high-performance wideband receivers and RF front-end building blocks, including low-noise amplifiers, mixers, and VGAs.
